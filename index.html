Nominate Your Workplace

How It Works

  1. Nominate your company.
  2. AudioEye performs a free digital accessibility audit.
  3. Audit results are emailed next week with a template on how to best share with your company.
  4. If your company partners with AudioEye we’ll donate $100 to Guide Dogs for the Blind in your name!

Why It Matters

  • 1 in 4 U.S. adults lives with a disability.
  • Over 70% of websites have accessibility barriers.
  • Accessibility boosts business and community inclusion.
  • Minimize risk and maximize compliance with a comprehensive accessibility solution .
